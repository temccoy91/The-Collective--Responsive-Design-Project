Our Mission

The Collective's mission is to educate and empower individuals battling with depression by providing the tools, resources, and skills to self-heal through music, exercise, building relationships, and meditation.

Featured Video of the Month
Guided Meditation by The Honest Guys

Practice meditation to calm anxiety and to clear your mind. Studies show that meditation can be just as effective as anti-depressants. Utilize this tool for a interactive meditation method. Yoga, listening to music, and sitting quietly in a private place can all be forms of meditation.
